Hotel Reservation System

1. Features of the Reservation System

Unordered List (Bulleted List)

  • Online booking of rooms.
  • Available room categories (Standard, Deluxe, Suite).
  • Multiple payment options (Credit Card, PayPal, etc.).
  • Booking confirmation and email notifications.
  • Customer support available 24/7.

Ordered List (Numbered List)

  1. Customer selects the room type and check-in/check-out dates.
  2. Customer enters personal details and payment information.
  3. System checks room availability and confirms the booking.
  4. Customer receives a booking confirmation email with all details.
  5. Customer can cancel or modify the reservation according to the policy.

2. Detailed Information About the Reservation System

Here is a table displaying important features and details of the Hotel Reservation System:

Feature Description Availability Price Range
Room Booking Allows users to book rooms online for a specified date range. Available for all users. $50 - $500 per night
Payment Options Supports multiple payment methods, including credit card and PayPal. Available after room selection. Varies based on room selection and booking service fees.
Cancellation Policy Users can cancel bookings with a full refund up to 48 hours before check-in. Available for bookings made more than 48 hours in advance. No additional charge for cancellations made within the policy.
Customer Support 24/7 customer support for reservations, cancellations, and inquiries. Always available. Free of charge
Booking Confirmation A confirmation email is sent after booking is completed, including the reservation details. Sent immediately after booking confirmation. No additional cost.
